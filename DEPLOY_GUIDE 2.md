# How to Deploy ThriftGH to Vercel (No Coding Required)

This guide gets your app live on the internet in under 30 minutes — for free.
Once live, anyone with a phone can visit the link and install it like an app.

---

## What You'll Need
- A laptop or computer
- A free GitHub account → github.com
- A free Vercel account → vercel.com

---

## Step 1 — Create a GitHub Account
1. Go to **github.com**
2. Click **Sign up** and create a free account
3. Verify your email

---

## Step 2 — Upload Your Project to GitHub
1. After logging in to GitHub, click the **+** button (top right) → **New repository**
2. Name it: `thriftgh`
3. Set it to **Public**
4. Click **Create repository**
5. On the next page, click **uploading an existing file**
6. Drag and drop ALL the files from the `thriftgh` folder I gave you
7. Click **Commit changes**

---

## Step 3 — Connect to Vercel
1. Go to **vercel.com** and click **Sign Up**
2. Choose **Continue with GitHub** — this links your accounts
3. Click **Add New Project**
4. Find your `thriftgh` repository and click **Import**
5. Vercel will auto-detect it's a Vite/React project
6. Leave all settings as default
7. Click **Deploy**

---

## Step 4 — Your App is Live! 🎉
- Vercel gives you a free link like: `thriftgh.vercel.app`
- Share this link with anyone — it works on all phones and computers
- Every time you update your code on GitHub, Vercel auto-updates the live site

---

## Step 5 — Get a Custom Domain (Optional, ~$15/year)
Want `thriftgh.com` instead of `thriftgh.vercel.app`?
1. Buy a domain on **Namecheap.com** (~$10–15/year)
2. In Vercel → your project → **Settings** → **Domains**
3. Add your domain and follow the instructions
4. Done — your app now has a professional address

---

## Step 6 — Tell Users to "Install" It
Once someone opens your link on their phone:

**On Android (Chrome):**
1. Tap the 3-dot menu (top right)
2. Tap **"Add to Home screen"**
3. App icon appears on their home screen ✅

**On iPhone (Safari):**
1. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow)
2. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. App icon appears on their home screen ✅
